## Usage Instructions

1. **Fill out during/immediately after session** while details are fresh
2. **Use user's actual words** for quotes whenever possible
3. **Be specific and concrete** rather than general
4. **Note what worked and didn't** for continuous improvement
5. **Flag interesting patterns** for research aggregation
6. **Maintain privacy** - anonymize if sharing for research

## Research Aggregation

Multiple session logs can be analyzed to:
- Identify common pattern combinations
- Assess technique effectiveness across users
- Refine the coaching workflow
- Develop pattern-specific intervention protocols
- Create user personas/archetypes
